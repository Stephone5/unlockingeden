The first project Unlocking Eden will focus on is Homes for Haiti.This project will be executed through the process of collecting trash, creating panels out of the waste, and then building homes out of the recycled building material. This would be done through a franchise with EcoDom- each home takes 7 days to build and is sold for $300. This partnership promises affordable homes, an increase in employment, and transformed streets. We believe a franchise with EcoDom is the most feasible way in meeting the needs for empowerment and sustainability within the nation of Haiti.
